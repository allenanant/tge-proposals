Red Flags Across The Pool

Verify before any offer

  • Sheet vs resume mismatches: Dhruvil is listed at 3 years but graduated in 2025. Sujay's stints overlap. Megha's year is annotation work, not engineering.
  • Percentage-metric inflation is endemic in this pool ("improved X by 35%", "reduced Y by 60%"). Treat every number as unverified until they walk through it live.
  • Nobody at this band walks in and runs the whole operation on day one. These are 1 to 3 year engineers. The hire is for trajectory + agentic-tooling fluency + autonomy, then ramp.
How To Decide

Do not decide off resumes. Run a paid trial task.

Give the top 3 the same one-day work sample, paid, using Claude Code. Whoever ships cleanest without hand-holding is the hire.
