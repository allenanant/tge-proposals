The Read
Observations from the past 14 days

You are running a respected clinic in a competitive market, but the paid layer is doing the work of organic. That is the gap.

Aspen Aesthetics has the operational fundamentals dialed in. The reviews on Google and the consistency on Instagram show a brand that takes itself seriously. The patient experience is clearly a 9 out of 10. None of that is the problem.

The problem is upstream. Meta and Google are being treated as awareness channels, not as a booking system. The ads run, the impressions land, the cost per lead climbs every month, and bookings stay flat. You are paying retail for traffic you should be converting on autopilot.

The opportunity is not to spend more. It is to rebuild the funnel underneath the spend so every dollar pushes a real booking, not a lurker.

The Plan
Three pillars, ninety days, one operator
01

Acquisition rebuild

Stop paying for awareness. Start paying for bookings.

  • Three ad concepts per service category, refreshed every 14 days
  • Geo-fenced campaigns within 18 miles of your Pearl St location
  • Lookalike audiences seeded from your last 12 months of bookings
  • Whitelisted creator content from two local micro-influencers
02

Conversion system

The moment after the click is where revenue is made.

  • Service-specific landing pages with calendar booking inline
  • SMS confirmation and one-click reschedule on the thank-you page
  • Social proof that matches the service the visitor came for
  • Pixel and CAPI hardening so retargeting actually works
03

Retention compounding

Your second booking is your most profitable booking.

  • Post-treatment SMS sequence keyed to each service protocol
  • Monthly client newsletter sent from your name, not the brand
  • Referral incentive that pays patients in credit, not cash
  • Quarterly returning patient campaign timed to filler half-life
04
Their Customer
Who the funnel is built for
Demographics

Women, 34 to 54, household income above $120K, residing within 18 miles of Boulder.

The most profitable segment in Boulder med spa is dual-income professional women in their late thirties and forties. Geo-density is highest in Niwot, Gunbarrel, and South Boulder.

Psychographics

They want to look like themselves, just rested, not augmented.

This audience reads the language of restraint. Subtle. Natural. Refreshed. Anything that hints at overdone or trend-chasing repels them.

Decision drivers

Trust the injector first, the brand second, the price last.

Bio detail and credentialing on the team page outperforms before-and-after grids. A real photo of the NP outconverts a stock shot 3 to 1.

Platforms

Instagram for discovery, Google for verification, your site for the booking.

The path is almost always: see ad on Instagram, search you on Google, read 3 reviews, click the website, book or bounce. Optimize for that path or lose the lead.
